Talk-O-Matic turns speech into text in real time, giving you a clear view of what's being said around you.

Whether you're hard of hearing, in a noisy environment, or simply want to follow along more easily, Talk-O-Matic works like subtitles for real life. As people speak, their words appear on your screen—instantly and accurately.

How it works

  • One device acts as the hub, capturing audio from the conversation
  • Other devices connect as clients to view the live transcription
  • Works over local network—no internet connection required
  • Clean, readable text that updates as people speak

Perfect for

  • Following group conversations at home, work, or social gatherings
  • Meetings, family dinners, or catching up with friends
  • Anyone who benefits from seeing words in addition to hearing them
  • Situations where you need a written record of what was said

Talk-O-Matic gives you the confidence to stay in the conversation, no matter where you are or who you're with.
